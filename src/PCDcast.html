PCDcast

This year PCD @Aarhus is producing a podcast series, as a way to connect with both local and global software art communities and coding scenes world wide. The podcast episodes will be presented in advance of Processing Community Day Aarhus, October 9th — and the indiviual episodes will be promoted on the Code&Share[ ] Instagram and Code&Share[ ] Facebook page
